Site Name

Rain or Shine

Site Purpose

This will be a weather site serving three towns in southeastern Idaho; Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven. It will contain current and forecasted weather conditions for each town. It will also have information about each town as well as current events and local attractions. The site will be used to plan day trips, camping and outdoor activities.
