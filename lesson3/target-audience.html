Target Audience

The target audience for Rain or Shine is tourists who plan to visit the area to sightsee, camp and do outdoor recreation.

Personas

Persona 1: Scoutmaster Pete Nelson

Profile:

  • Age - 42
  • Income - $65,000/year
  • Employment - I.T. Professional
  • Educations - Bachelor's Degree
  • Marital Status - Married with kids
  • Hobbies - Camping, computers, guitar

Pete loves the great outdoors. His dream was to be the scoutmaster of an active scout troop. He is a master of building shelters using materials found in nature. He is very tech savy and uses a computer, laptop, tablet and cellphone regularly. He doesn't mind getting wet but is not a fan of low temperatures. He takes his young son along with him on all his scout trips with his troop. He likes to play his guitar on camping trips but doesn't like his guitar to get wet.

Persona 2: Mary Mitchell

Profile:

  • Age - 35
  • Income - $42,000/year
  • Employment - Administrative Assistant
  • Educations - Some College
  • Marital Status - Single Mother with kids
  • Hobbies - Reading, macrame, archery. Enjoys books and attending kid's soccer games and school activities.

Mary works hard to support her kids and she wants them to experience the adventure of the great outdoors. That said, she does not love camping. She prefers to go out on a Saturday and experience nature through a hike in the forest or a picnic by the lake. She does like to get out and shoot her bow and arrows and she's teaching her kids her love of archery. She doesn't like temperature extremes and she likes to be home by dark.

Scenarios

Here are five scenarios this site would be valuable for.

  1. Site visitor wants to know what the weather will be for a weekend overnight camping trip.
  2. Site visitor wants to know what weekend activities are happening in the town they are planning to visit.
  3. Site visitor wants to know the weather lakeside on a Saturday afternoon.
  4. Site visitor wants to check for emergency weather conditions as they are heading out on their fishing trip.
  5. Site visitor wants to know what kids activities are happening in the area around where they are camping.
