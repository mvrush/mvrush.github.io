Our Purpose

Thank you for visiting the Pleasant Grove Chamber of Commerce. We are here to help you get to know the businesses and business environment of Pleasant Grove. Membership in our Chamber of Commerce brings the benifit of placing your business among the elite businesses in Pleasant Grove.

Pleasant Grove is renowned for it's business presence throughout the world. From small local businesses like Chubby's Neighborhood Cafe to huge multi-national corporations like dôTERRA Pleasant Grove has it all.

We are happy that you are learning more about our wonderful city and organization. We welcome you to Pleasant Grove and invite you to explore our city. Visit our business directory to see some of the thriving businesses that call Pleasant Grove home.
