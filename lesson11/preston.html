Napoleon Comes Home

Napoleon Dynamite returned home today after a grueling 7 months at Preston Community College. Napoleon said, "The college didn't have dorms so I just lived in a tent out behind the dumpster until I got my degree."

Napoleon's degree is in Agricultural Animal Husbandry. Mr. Dynamite said, "I'm going to create a new breed of chickens. The breed will have much smaller talons. In my previous work, I was pretty scratched up by the chicken's large talons and I really want to solve that problem".

For now Napoleon Dynamite plans to take the summer off and spend time with his uncle in Alaska hunting wolverines. Mr. Dyamite said, "Last summer I shot, like 50 wolverines! This year I'll be gunning for 100. I'm planning on using a twelve-guage to keep my cousins safe from the wolverines. There is nothing more frightening than a wolverine attacking a child."

Napoleon Dynamite was alone in his graduating class. On his degree it says "Preston Community College, Advanced Animal Husbandry Certificate". The degree was signed by Preston Community College President Rico Dynamite, Napoleon's uncle.
